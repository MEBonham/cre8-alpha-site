New Around Here?

If you're completely new to tabletop RPGs, check out RPG Basics.

In general, watch out for green sidebar panels—they're especially for newcomers.

Looking for GM Ideas?

The Context section is where settings and modules will eventually be.

Gold sidebars like this one will contain helpful links, sometimes to things like tables of contents or examples of CRE8 play.

Other Sidebar Colors?

Red sidebars contain advanced rules or clarifications. Don't worry about them at first.

Gray sidebars are miscellaneous things. Sometimes reminders the website creator leaves for himself, sometimes tips for the GM, sometimes tech support suggestions.
