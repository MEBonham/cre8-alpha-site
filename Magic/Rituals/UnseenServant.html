Unseen Servant

[Arcane] Ritual

Level: 1
Copyable? Yes
Casting Time:

1 minute

Standard Component Cost: 6
MP Cost: 1
Range: 30 ft
Duration:

Permanent unless destroyed.

Effect:

This ritual creates a mindless entity of invisible force to do its creator's bidding. This entity does not speak or fight, and it is destroyed by any effect that is capable of dealing VP damage to it. It is typically used for light manual labor or impressing spectators, but can also be useful as a type of telekinesis. The servant is about the size of its master and can lift up to 10 pounds or drag up to 30 pounds of objects. The master commands the entity mentally; issuing a new command is a standard action, while maintaining a previous command is a swift action each round.

The servant moves with a Speed of 5, and if the servant is ever more than 30 feet from its master, it ceases to exist. However, if it is destroyed in this manner, its master may perform this ritual again without paying the standard component cost.

Variations

Some ritualists prefer to have servants that are bigger (mostly taller) than themselves. Such variations normally come with a slight increase in component costs.
