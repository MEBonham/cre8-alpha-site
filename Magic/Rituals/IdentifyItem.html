Identify Item

[Arcane] [Divination] Ritual

Level: 1
Copyable? Yes
Casting Time:

15 minutes

Standard Component Cost: 7
MP Cost: 1
Range: touch
Target:

One object

Effect:

You learn the magical properties, if any, of the target, up to a certain point. Abilities of the item that require no attunement (e.g. properties of consumables), perks, and first-echelon abilities are generally discerned. So are curses, except for some powerful curses that have particularly been designed to evade this divinatory power.

This ritual is not a reliable way to gather information about quirks or higher-echelon main functions of magic items. The GM can reveal these details about an item at her own discretion (i.e. if she has already decided what higher-echelon abilities an item has, and she doesn't think discovering them gradually would improve the story; or if she wishes to advance the plot by giving out hints about a specific quirk that has been puzzling the players). If she is undecided about revealing higher-echelon abilities, she may (secretly) roll the ritualist's Spellcraft check and consult the table provided.
