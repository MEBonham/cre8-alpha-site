Base!

Unlike most bonuses to saving throws, this talent increases your base Fortitude save. Thus, it can be very useful for qualifying for other abilities that have a Fortitude-based prerequisite.

Mighty Constitution

[Core] Talent

Prerequisites:

Heroic Bonus +1.

Benefits:
  • If Fortitude is your good save (as determined by the first step of character creation), you no longer auto-fail Fortitude saves on a natural 1, and peril is never triggered on an attack that you oppose with Fortitude.
  • Otherwise (if Reflex or Willpower is your good save), gain a +1 bonus to your base Fortitude save. At Level 5, this bonus increases to +2.
