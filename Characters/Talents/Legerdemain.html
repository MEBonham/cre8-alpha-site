Legerdemain

[Core] [Skill Trick] Talent

Prerequisites:

Coast Number 7; Dexterity 2 ranks.

Benefit:

You can make uncapped Dexterity checks to lift a carried item off of a person or to perform sleight of hand tricks.

Also, if you make obvious contact with someone while lifting an item off of them, but downplay it as incidental contact, the TN for them to immediately notice the theft is either the result of your Dexterity check or the result of your Glibness check, whichever is higher.

Normal

Attempts to perform sleight of hand or lift a carried item off of a person are capped. Glibness does not help you prevent such an attempt from being noticed.
