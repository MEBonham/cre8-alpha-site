Speak Language

[Core] [Skill Trick] Talent

Select a language from your game's campaign setting.

Prerequisites: none.
Benefit:

The in-game time for your character to be functional in the selected language is greatly reduced:

Level of Understanding Time Without Talent Time With Talent Time With Talent and Knowledge 2 Ranks
Basic (skill checks at disadvantage & capped) 6 months 1 month 1 day
Fluent (skill checks capped) 2 years 8 months 4 months
Mastery (no penalties) 15 years 5 years 2 years

To count towards these thresholds, in-game time must be spent immersed day-to-day in the new language or actively studying it.

Special:

You may take this talent more than once. Each time you take this talent, it applies to a different language.
