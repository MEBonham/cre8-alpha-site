Illiteracy

[Background] [Flaw] Talent

Prerequisites: none.
Benefit:

You do not trigger magical traps that are triggered by reading.

Drawbacks:
  • Knowledge -1
  • You cannot read or write.
Special:

In campaign settings where literacy is not common, the GM may give this talent out to all characters by default, and require spending a Talent in order to learn to read and write.
