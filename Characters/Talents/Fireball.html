Fireball

[Core] [Metamagic] Talent

Prerequisites:

Caster Level 3; Energy Blast feat; Fire Seed talent.

Benefit:

When you cast Energy Blast using the Fire seed, you may expend momentum to alter the parameters of the spell:

  • Base MP Cost: 3 MP
  • Target: all creatures and objects within up to 3 sectors that are all adjacent to each other. This cannot be further augmented with more targets.
  • Base impact of attack: 2d6+1d4. This cannot be further augmented.
  • Range of attack: 60/150/400 ft.
