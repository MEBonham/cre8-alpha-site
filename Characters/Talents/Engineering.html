Engineering

[Core] [Skill Trick] Talent

Prerequisites: none.
Benefits:
  • You can use your Knowledge skill (uncapped) to answer questions about architectural features or styles.
  • You can use your Gadgetry skill (uncapped) to build machinery. You can coast checks to see if a structure you built is sound.
  • During downtime, you can use your Gadgetry skill to earn a steady income.
