Peformer

[Core] [Skill Trick] Talent

Prerequisites:

Coast Number 7; (base Reflex +2 or Glibness 1 rank).

Benefits:
  • Your skill checks are uncapped when their primary purpose is to impress an audience, e.g. when using Athletics to represent dancing, or using Glibness to represent giving an oratory or comedic performance.
  • During downtime, you may use Athletics, Brawn, Dexterity, or Glibness to earn a steady income.
Special

If you have a talent representing ability to play a family of musical instruments, you can coast rolls for performances with those instruments.

Normal

Impressing an audience is sufficiently different from normal uses of a skill to cause that skill's checks to be capped.
