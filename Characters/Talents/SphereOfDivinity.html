Sphere of Divinity

Talent

Prerequisites:

Base Willpower +3; Lore of Divinity talent; one [Spell] feat; one Kit (see Special), one [Seed] talent (see Special).

Special:

This talent gives you an advantage as a consequence of having a bond with a specific religious tradition. The Kit and Talent specified in the prerequisites must be choices that fit with the selected religion. The religions available to your character are determined by the campaign setting you are playing in. For many religions, the Kit in the prerequisites must be the Priest kit, but some religions allow other Kits, such as Shaman or Wizard or Warlock, to qualify. (In some settings, the gods may even choose their own favorites without any input from the mortal in question; in such a case, the Lore of Divinity talent might not be required, at the GM's discretion.) If your chosen religion has more than one Seed in its "portfolio," and you have more than one of the Seeds in question, choose one of them when you take this talent.

Benefit:

Once per round, when you cast a spell with the selected Seed that is in your religion's portfolio, you may attempt a TN 22 Charisma check as a free action. If this check succeeds, gain momentum.

Special:

If you have Caster Level 5 and an additional Seed from your religion's portfolio, you may take this talent a second time for that Seed. If you have Caster Level 5 and you are Level 9 or higher, and you have a third Seed from your religion's portfolio, you may take this talent a third time for the third Seed in question. You still can only use this talent once per round.
