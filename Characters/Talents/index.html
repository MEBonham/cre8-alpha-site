Talents

Talents are lesser abilities, but still things that your character is good at that not everyone is good at. Many talents add to what your character can do with their Skills, or define what types of magic or weaponry they are familiar with.

Sometimes Talents round out your character, demonstrating that they have a life outside of adventuring (or used to). Other times, Talents contribute to your character's adventuring style.

Core Talents

The following is a list of the [Core] Talent options that are expected to cover most PCs in most campaigns, broken into a number of categories. You can also view a list of all Talents, or (below the [Core] list) find more lists of Talents filtered in various ways.
