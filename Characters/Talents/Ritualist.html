Ritualist

[Core] Talent

Prerequisites:

Caster Level 1; (Lore of Arcana talent or Lore of Divinity talent or (Knowledge 3 ranks; Wild Empathy talent)); must be literate.

Benefit:

You may attempt to perform any ritual from a scroll or ritual book.

Normal:

To perform a ritual, you must have a talent matching one of its keywords, such as Lore of Arcana for [Arcane] rituals.
