Multiple Resolutions

If a creature is targeted more than once by this ability during a single initiative roll, all but one instance of the ability are ineffectual. The target can choose which instance of the ability goes into effect.

A creature using this talent is immune to foes' Staredowns, except if she and a foe target each other mutually. In that case, they are both considered to have selected the "Confront" option.

Staredown

[Core] Talent

Prerequisites:

Fighting Level 1 or Brawn 3 ranks.

Benefit:

When you and a foe you can see roll intiative, you may target that foe with an attempt to intimidate them before the conflict begins in earnest. The target must choose one of the following options:

  • Ignore. You make a Brawn or Charisma check. The result of that check becomes the TN of a Willpower save from the target. If this save fails, you gain momentum.
  • Confront. You make a Brawn or Charisma check; your target has the same choice. Whoever has the lower result incurs a -10 penalty to their initiative score in the conflict. (In the case of a tie, there is no effect.)
