Kits

Kits are the "packages" of abilities that tie your character together. They fill the role of Classes from some other RPGs: they're your character's unifying themes and archetypes.

Core Kits

The following is a list of the [Core] Kit options that are expected to cover most PCs in most campaigns. You can also view a list of all kits, or (below the [Core] list) find lists of Kits filtered in various ways.

Assassin
