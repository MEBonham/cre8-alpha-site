Magician

[Core] Kit

Prerequisites:

Caster Level 2; Dexterity 1 rank; Knowledge 3 ranks; Lore of Arcana talent; one [Seed] talent.

Benefits:
  • Caster Level +1
  • Gain two bonus [Seed] talents.
  • You may switch between active seeds as a free action.
  • You may use your Spellcraft check in place of your Perception skill to disbelieve illusions.
Drawback:

Arcane Aversion: Your encumbrance penalty applies to your Spellcraft check and the accuracy of your spell attacks. Increase this penalty by 1 if you wear any armor, and again by 1 if you wear a shield.

XP Parcels:
  • Dexterity 5 ranks; Performer talent.
  • Anyseed talent; Ritualist talent.
