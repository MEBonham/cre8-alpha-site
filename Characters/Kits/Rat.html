Rat

[Racial] [Monster] Kit

Prerequisites:

No other [Racial] Kits.

Benefits:
  • Tiny Size: accuracy +2 with weapon attacks; Defense +2; situational +/-5.
  • Gain a +1 racial bonus to Speed.
  • Gain a +2 racial bonus to Dexterity.
  • Bite: Gain a natural weapon (base impact 1d4, piercing, peril +2, melee reach 0).
  • Low-Light Vision: Treat nonmagical dim light as if it were bright light.
Drawbacks:
  • Tiny Size: Brawn -4; AV -2 (size penalty); weapon impact -2 (size penalty); Speed -2 (size penalty); melee reach -1.5; situational +/-5.
  • Animal Type:
    • Cannot speak most languages. Illiterate.
    • Limit the Charisma, Dexterity, Gadgetry, Glibness, and Knowledge skills.
