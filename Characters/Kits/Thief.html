Thief

[Core] Kit

Prerequisites:

Dexterity 2 ranks or Stealth 2 ranks.

Benefits:
  • Coast Number +1
  • Gain two bonus [Skill Trick] talents.
  • Train an additional skill.
  • You may coast Dexterity checks, and Stealth checks to hide or sneak, even under pressure.
XP Parcels:
  • Cutpurse: Dexterity 3 ranks; Legerdemain talent.
  • Con Artist: Rogue kit; Snake-Tongued talent.
  • Cat Burglar: Athletics 3 ranks; Gadgetry 2 ranks; Lockpicking talent; Steady Climber talent.
