Halfling

[Core] [Racial] Kit

Prerequisites:

No other [Racial] Kits.

Benefits:
  • Small Size: accuracy +1 with weapon attacks; Defense +1; situational +/-5.
  • Gain a +1 racial bonus to Speed.
  • Train Dexterity. (If Dexterity is already trained, you may train another skill.)
  • Oddly Fearless: You have advantage on saves vs. [Fear] effects.
  • You are proficient with using thrown rocks as weapons; their ranges increase to 15/30/45 ft.
Drawbacks:
  • Small Size: Brawn -2; AV -1 (size penalty); weapon impact -1 (size penalty); Speed -1 (size penalty); melee reach -0.5; situational +/-5.
XP Parcels:
  • Dagger Training talent or Sling Training talent.
  • Legerdemain talent.
  • Gypsy: Nature 2 ranks; Stealth 3 ranks; (Rogue kit or Shaman kit or Shadow Seed talent); Wanderer talent.
  • Athletics 3 ranks; Dodge feat; Evasion talent; (Mighty Leap talent or Steady Climber talent).
  • Coast Number 9; Glibness 4 ranks; one [Luck] feat.
  • Simple Wisdom talent.
  • Brawler kit; Cavalier kit; Animal Bond feat.
