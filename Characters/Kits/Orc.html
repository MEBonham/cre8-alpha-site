Orc

[Core] [Racial] Kit

Prerequisites:

No other [Racial] Kits.

Benefits:
  • Gain a +1 feat bonus to accuracy of weapon attacks.
  • Train Brawn. (If Brawn is already trained, you may train another skill.)
  • Bully: You have advantage on intimidation checks.
  • Low-Light Vision: Treat nonmagical dim light as if it were bright light.
Drawbacks:

Incur a -2 penalty to Glibness.

XP Parcels:
  • Nature 4 ranks; Wild Empathy talent.
  • Favored Enemy talent (elves or dwarves).
  • Brawler kit or Ruffian kit or Shaman kit.
  • Furious Rage feat.
