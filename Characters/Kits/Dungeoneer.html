Dungeoneer

[Core] Kit

Prerequisites:

Coast Number 8; Athletics 4 ranks; Gadgetry 2 ranks; Perception 3 ranks; Trapfinder talent.

Benefits:
  • Coast Number +1
  • VP +2
  • Your Knowledge skill applies (uncapped) to questions about dungeon architecture, aberrations, and oozes.
  • You can use your Awesome check in place of your Gadgetry skill to bypass traps. You can still coast such rolls.
  • You have advantage on all saving throws against traps.
XP Parcels:
  • Escape Artist talent.
  • Lockpicking talent.
  • Favored Enemy (aberrations and oozes) talent.
  • Mighty Leap talent; Steady Climber talent.
  • Gadgetry 5 ranks; Improvisation feat.
  • Gadgetry 3 ranks; Knowledge 3 ranks; Appraisal talent; (Loremaster kit or Haggler talent).
  • Fighting Level 3; Athletics 6 ranks; Dexterity 3 ranks; Whip Training talent.
  • Knowledge 5 ranks; (Engineering talent or Lore of History talent).
