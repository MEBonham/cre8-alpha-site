Factotum

[Core] Kit

Prerequisites:

Coast Number 10; any skill 7 ranks; Improvisation feat.

Benefits:
  • Fighting Level +1
  • Coast Number +1
  • Caster Level +1
  • VP +2
  • Train an additional skill.
  • By exerting as a free action, or when you use the Improvisation feat, or when you take a rest, you may retrain one [Skill Trick] talent into another [Skill Trick] talent.
XP Parcels:
  • Knowledge 5 ranks; (Bard kit or Dungeoneer kit or Loremaster kit).
  • Anyspell feat; (Magician kit or Anyseed talent).
