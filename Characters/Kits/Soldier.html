Retraining Note

You may not retrain all other sources of martial weapon proficiency and leave only this kit, since one of those sources of martial weapon proficiency was used as a prerequisite to enter this kit.

Soldier

[Core] Kit

Prerequisites:

(Base Fortitude +1 or Brawn 2 ranks); Athletics 1 rank; proficient with a martial weapon.

Benefits:
  • Fighting Level +1 or Reserve Points +1
  • VP +4
  • Martial Weaponry: You are proficient with all martial weapons and with light armor. (You may immediately retrain Light Armor Proficiency.)
  • You can coast Knowledge checks to know about military hierarchies and policies.
XP Parcels:
