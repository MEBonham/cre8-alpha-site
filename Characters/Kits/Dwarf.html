Dwarf

[Core] [Racial] Kit

Prerequisites:

No other [Racial] Kits.

Benefits:
  • Gain a +2 racial bonus to Fortitude saves.
  • Ignore the Speed penalty for wearing heavy armor (including chainmail), as long as you're proficient.
  • You have advantage on saving throws vs. poison or intoxication.
  • Stability: You have advantage on stability checks.
  • Darkvision: Treat nonmagical darkness as if it were dim light.
Drawbacks:
  • Incur a -1 penalty to Speed.
  • Incur a -2 penalty to Glibness.
XP Parcels:
  • Gadgetry 3 ranks; (Armorsmith talent or Engineering talent or Weaponsmith talent).
  • Base Fortitude +3; Gadgetry 2 ranks; Artisan (brewer) talent.
  • Favored Enemy talent (giants or goblinoids or orcs).
  • Fighting Level 3; Charisma 3 ranks; (Battle Banter feat or Staredown talent or Bully trait).
  • Axe Master kit or Hammer Master kit.
