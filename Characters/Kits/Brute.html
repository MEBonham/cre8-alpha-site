Brute

[Core] Kit

Prerequisites:

Base Fortitude +2; ((Fighting Level 2; Brawn 5 ranks) or Large Size (or larger)).

Benefits:
  • Fighting Level +1
  • VP +4
  • Gain a +1 feat bonus to the impact of your melee weapon attacks.
  • When you select a hazard to inflict, you qualify for Hazard Menu options as if you were one size category larger.
  • Gain a Brawn-based synergy bonus to your Awesome check.
XP Parcels:
  • Power Attack feat.
