Elf

[Core] [Racial] Kit

Prerequisites:

No other [Racial] Kits.

Benefits:
  • Gain a +2 racial bonus to Reflex saves.
  • Gain a +1 racial bonus to Speed while not wearing heavy armor.
  • Gain a +1 racial bonus to Spellcraft.
  • Train Perception. (If Perception is already trained, you may train another skill.)
  • You have advantage on saves vs. [Charm] effects.
  • You may spend half of an extended rest doing calm activities (such as keeping watch, reading, meditating, or hand crafts), rather than being inert during that time.
  • Low-Light Vision: Treat nonmagical dim light as if it were bright light.
Drawbacks:
  • Incur a -2 size penalty penalty to Brawn.
  • Incur a -1 size penalty to AV.
  • Incur a -1 size penalty to weapon impact.
XP Parcels:
  • Proficiency (light blades) talent or Proficiency (heavy blades) talent or Proficiency (bows) talent.
  • Nature 3 ranks; Lightfoot Step feat.
  • Athletics 4 ranks; Nature 1 rank; Steady Climber talent.
  • Base Reflex +4; Sage Spellcasting talent.
