Radiant

[Core] Kit

Prerequisites:

Caster Level 2; Charisma 1 rank; (Energy Blast feat or Energy Strike feat or Healing Touch feat); Radiant Seed talent.

Benefits:
  • Caster Level +1
  • VP +2 or gain a bonus [Buff] talent.
  • You can cast spells using the Radiant seed even if it is not your currently active seed.
  • When you have the Radiant Seed active and you cast Energy Blast, Energy Strike, or Healing Touch using it, recycle 1 MP.
XP Parcels:
  • Low-Light Vision trait.
