Champion

[Core] Kit

Prerequisites:

Fighting Level 4; (Energy Strike feat or Smite feat); proficient with light armor.

Benefits:
  • Fighting Level +1
  • Caster Level +1
  • VP +4
  • Gain a Charisma-based synergy bonus to all saves except Defense.
XP Parcels:
  • Proficient with heavy armor.
  • Lore of Divinity talent.
  • Radiate Aura feat.

Variation: Paladin

A paladin is a Champion who lives by a code that espouses honor and virtue to an extreme degree. The details of this code of honor are setting-dependent and should be worked out with your GM. In addition to the normal properties of the Champion kit, the Paladin has the following:

  • Benefit - Blight-Defiant: If you are blight-snared, ignore the worst two stages of its effects. Your saving throws against new stages of blight are never capped due to the blight's other effects.
  • Drawback: If you seriously violate your code of honor, you lose your Blight-Defiant trait and your synergy bonus to saves until you atone for your misdeeds.
  • XP Parcel: Exorcism feat.
