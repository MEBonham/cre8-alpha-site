Wizard

[Core] Kit

Prerequisites:

Knowledge 2 ranks; Lore of Arcana talent; must be literate.

Benefits:
  • Caster Level +1
  • Gain a bonus [Buff] talent or [Seed] talent.
  • Gain a Knowledge-based synergy bonus to your MP Pool.
  • When you take an extended rest with access to a spellbook, you can retrain one [Spell] feat. The new feat must also be a [Spell] feat, and must be inscribed in the spellbook.
Drawback:

Arcane Aversion: Your encumbrance penalty applies to your Spellcraft check and the accuracy of your spell attacks. Increase this penalty to these stats by 1 if you wear any armor, and again by 1 if you wear a shield.

XP Parcels:
  • Gadgetry 3 ranks; (Shaman kit or Tinker kit); Alchemy talent.
