This is how many Experience Points your character has. Experience Points track your character's progress towards a higher level.
Your level is a measure of your character's overall power and advancement. Level 1 is very inexperienced, like a typical adolescent. Level 4 is the maximum for "normal" people. Level 8 makes you one of the greatest heroes in the world.

Your character has four Saving Throws they can roll to determine how well they do defensively. Fortitude measures your health and stamina. Your base Fortitude save also affects your Vitality Points maximum.
Your character has four Saving Throws they can roll to determine how well they do defensively. Reflex is used to see how fast you can close your eyes or avoid falling in a pit. It also determines your Initiative scores.
Your character has four Saving Throws they can roll to determine how well they do defensively. Willpower measures mental defense. It also helps you recharge your Magic Points without getting tired as fast.
Your character has four Saving Throws they can roll to determine how well they do defensively. Defense is the one you use against most attacks, whether they be a sword or a dragon's fire breath. Wielding a shield greatly improves your Defense Save.
Armor Value measures how tough your character is when actually struck. It can be improved by wearing armor, of course, or by being larger or having better Brawn.
When you are struck by an effect that you are resistant to, use your Resistance Value instead of your Armor Value for resolving that effect's outcome.
Heroic Bonus is a statistic to roll when nothing else applies, or when you're not very good at something.
Spellcraft measures how good you are at controlling your magic and using it in precise ways.
Your Awesome Check is a statistic for you to use with Roll Replacement when you're really good at something.
When you coast a skill check, this number is the minimum effective die roll you can get.
Athletics is a Skill measuring how well you can do physical stunts, including climbing or jumping.
Glibness is a Skill measuring how persuasive you are at fast-talking.
Brawn is a Skill measuring how strong you are in a challenge of brute force.
Knowledge is a Skill measuring how much you know or remember.
Charisma is a Skill measuring how much you are able to build up relationships over time.
Nature is a Skill measuring how well you can survive in the wilds.
Dexterity is a Skill measuring your fine motor skills, especially with your hands.
Perception is a Skill measuring how aware you are of your surroundings.
Gadgetry is a Skill measuring your knack for working with items or machines.
Stealth is a Skill measuring how well you can go unnoticed.
