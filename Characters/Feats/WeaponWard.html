Weapon Ward

[Core] [Spell] Feat

Prerequisites:

Caster Level 1.

Action: Standard
Base MP Cost: 0 MP
Seeds:

Air, Cold, Earth, Fire, Illusory, Lightning, Luck, Mind, Plant, Shadow

Target:

One creature.

Range:

Touch (i.e. within your fray).

Duration:

Encounter, or until expended

Base Effect:

The target is warded; it may use your Spellcraft check in place of a Defense save against a weapon attack. For this effect to work, you must spend 1 MP at the time of use, which does not require an action.

If the replaced Defense save succeeds, or if you do not spend 1 MP when the target makes a Defense save, the ward ends for that target unless you spend an opportunity action to sustain it.

Augment:
  • MP +1: Cast this spell as a swift action. You must be the sole target of the spell.
  • MP +1: Increase range to within your sector.
  • MP +2: The target rolls both its own Defense save and your Spellcraft check, and uses the better of the two results.
  • MP +3: Target a number of creatures equal to your Spellcraft modifier. All targets must be within your sector.
Seed Effects:
  • Any (MP +2): This spell may be used regardless of active seed, but at an increased cost.
  • Air: This spell's ward can only be applied against ranged attacks.
    • MP +2: The ward works against melee attacks as normal.
  • Cold: An attacker who makes a melee attack against the target takes incidental cold damage equal to your Heroic Bonus.
    • MP +1: When the target uses this effect against a weapon attack that deals fire damage, she can also resist the attack. This expends the ward regardless of outcome (although it can still be sustained).
  • Earth:
    • MP +1: When the target uses the ward, she can also resist the attack. This expends the ward regardless of outcome (although it can still be sustained).
  • Fire: An attacker who makes a melee attack against the target takes incidental fire damage equal to your Heroic Bonus.
    • MP +1: When the target uses the ward against a weapon attack that deals cold damage, she can also resist the attack. This expends the ward regardless of outcome (although it can still be sustained).
  • Illusory: The ward cannot be used against an attacker who is blinded, closing its eyes, or otherwise unable to see the target.
    • MP +2: As long as the ward lasts, the target also has concealment against all weapon attacks.
  • Lightning (MP +1): An attacker who makes a melee attack against the target takes incidental lightning damage equal to your Heroic Bonus.
  • Luck: The ward is not expended by a successful use; instead, the ward is expended (unless sustained) if the target fails its save while using the effect.
  • Mind (+1): Using this spell with the Mind Seed works normally, but at a higher cost.
  • Plant:
    • MP +2: Sustaining the spell is a free action.
  • Shadow: Using this spell with the Shadow Seed works normally.
Benefit:

Gain a +1 feat bonus to your Spellcraft check.
