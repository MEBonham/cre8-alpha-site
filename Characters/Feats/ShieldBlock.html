Shield Block

[Core] [Counter] Feat

Prerequisites:

Fighting Level 2.

Action: Opportunity (expend momentum)
Trigger:

You are wielding a shield when an ally in your fray is about to roll a Defense save.

Effect:

Roll a Defense save; the triggering ally uses whichever Defense result is higher. Regardless of which result is used, if the save succeeds, the triggering ally resists the attack's effects.
