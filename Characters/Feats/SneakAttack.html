Sneak Attack

[Core] [Strike] Feat

Prerequisites:

Coast Number 7; Stealth 3 ranks.

Action: Standard
Effect:

Make a weapon attack.

If the target of your attack has not yet acted in this encounter, it is off-guard against your attack.

If the target of your attack is surprised, dazed, or stunned, or if you were hidden from the target at the start of your turn, your attack adds +1d4 impact. Special: If the attack is a melee attack made with a light blade weapon, it instead adds +1d6 impact.

If your attack inflicts a hazard during a surprise round, make an Awesome check (TN 20) to gain momentum.
