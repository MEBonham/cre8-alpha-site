Call Ally

[Core] [Spell] Feat

Prerequisites:

Caster Level 2; applicable seed.

Action: Standard
Base MP Cost: 2 MP
Seeds:

Air, Animal, Cold, Earth, Fire, Lightning, Mind, Plant, Radiant, Shadow, Water

Range:

Within your fray.

Duration:

Encounter

Base Effect:

Create a Level 1 stooge within range as a [Conjuration] effect. This stooge will consider you an ally.

If you can communicate with the stooge and spend actions to direct it, it will generally cooperate. Otherwise, it will act on its own initiative, as controlled by the GM, which likely involves doing little unless its self-preservation is threatened.

You can only have one stooge at a time.

Augment:
  • MP +1 (cumulative): Increase the Level of the stooge by 1, to a maximum of 9. (Find base statistics on the stooge table.)
  • MP +1: Increase range to anywhere within your sector.
  • MP +2: Increase range to anywhere within adjacent sectors.
  • MP +2: Conjure a Swarm of stooges instead of a single stooge.
  • MP +2: The stooge can automatically understand you.
  • MP +3: Increase range to line of sight.
  • MP +4: The stooge has its own full set of actions, immediately before you in the initiative order, and can be directed as a free action.
Seed Effects:
  • Air (MP +1): The conjured stooge is formed of ambient air, can fly, and understands Auran. It is extraplanar, and returns to its home plane when dropped (or when spell duration expires).
    • MP +1: As a Menu (B) option, the conjured stooge may be completely invisible.
  • Animal: The conjured stooge must emulate an animal plentiful in local wilderness. It can communicate like an animal.
    • MP +1: The stooge can be an animal from anywhere; no local wilderness needed. It is extraplanar, and returns to its home plane (Feywild) when dropped (or when spell duration expires).
  • Cold: The conjured stooge is formed of ambient snow or ice. Its attacks deal cold damage, in addition to their normal type. As a Menu (A) option, the stooge can resist cold damage.
    • MP +1: The requirement for ambient cold materials is waived. The stooge is extraplanar, and returns to its home plane when dropped (or when spell duration expires).
  • Earth: The conjured stooge is formed of ambient stone or soil, does not need to breathe, and understands Terran. It is extraplanar, and returns to its home plane when dropped (or when spell duration expires). As a Menu (B) option, the stooge may have the ability to burrow.
    • MP +1: The requirement for ambient earth is waived.
  • Fire: The conjured stooge is formed of ambient flames, and understands Ignan. Its attacks deal fire damage, in addition to their normal type. When the stooge appears, deal incidental fire damage (equal to your Heroic Bonus) to other creatures in its fray. As a Menu (A) option, the stooge can resist fire damage.
    • MP +1: The requirement for ambient fire is waived. The stooge is extraplanar, and returns to its home plane when dropped (or when spell duration expires).
  • Lightning (MP +1): The conjured stooge's attacks deal lightning damage, in addition to their normal type, and deal +1d6 impact. As a Menu (A) option, the stooge can fly. At the start of your turn, the spell ends unless you sustain it (swift action, 1 MP).
  • Mind: The stooge understands you telepathically. It is a construct, with Hit Points rather than Vitality Points.
  • Plant: The conjured stooge is formed from ambient plants, and cannot move. It understands you, but only if you succeed on a TN 10 Spellcraft check each time you give it directions.
    • MP +1: The requirement of ambient plant life is waived. The stooge is extraplanar, and returns to its home plane (Feywild) when dropped (or when spell duration expires).
    • MP +1: The stooge can move, but its Speed is 3 (before Menu options are applied).
  • Radiant (MP +2): The stooge is extraplanar, and returns to its home plane when dropped (or when spell duration expires). It understands Celestial, and has the Blight-Defiant trait. If it can fly (as a Menu (B) option), it also gains a bonus Menu (A) option.
    • MP +1: The stooge's attacks can deal radiance damage instead of their normal damage type.
  • Shadow: The stooge is formed from an ambient corpse. It is undead, with Hit Points rather than Vitality Points. It understands you, but only if you succeed on a TN 10 Spellcraft check each time you give it directions.
    • MP +1: The stooge's attacks can deal radiance damage instead of their normal damage type.
    • MP +3: The requirement of a corpse is waived. The stooge has the Incorporeal trait. It is extraplanar, and returns to its home plane (Shadowfel) when dropped (or when spell duration expires).
  • Water: The conjured stooge is formed of ambient water, has the swim movement mode, does not need to breathe, and understands Aquan. It is extraplanar, and returns to its home plane when dropped (or when spell duration expires).
    • MP +1: The requirement of an ambient body of water is waived.
