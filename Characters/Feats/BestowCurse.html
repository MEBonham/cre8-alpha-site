Bestow Curse

[Core] [Spell] Feat

Prerequisites:

Caster Level 1; applicable seed.

Action: Standard
Base MP Cost: 1 MP
Seeds:

Animal, Illusory, Luck, Mind, Shadow

Target:

One creature within range.

Duration:

Encounter

Base Effect:

Make a spell attack against the target(s): impact 1d8, necrotic damage, peril +0, range 20/40/60 ft. This attack is opposed by a Willpower save. If this attack inflicts a hazard, select it from the provided menu instead of the normal Hazard Menu.

Augment:
  • MP +0: Reduce base impact to 1d4. Compare impact to Heroic Bonus, rather than AV, to determine secondary effects.
  • MP +1 (cumulative): Reduce base impact to 1d4. Compare impact to Heroic Bonus, rather than AV, to determine secondary effects. Add an additional target. All targets must be within one sector.
  • MP +2: Add +1d4 impact.
  • MP +2: Increase duration to 3 days.
  • MP +3: Increase duration to permanent.
Seed Effects:
  • Any (MP +2): This spell may be used regardless of active seed, but at an increased cost.
  • Animal: If the target would be dropped by this spell, it is instead changed into a small, helpless animal for the duration of the spell. This is a [Shapeshift] effect.
  • Illusory: The target has advantage on its save against this attack. This spell is a [Fear] effect.
    • MP +1: The spell no longer counts as a [Fear] effect.
    • MP +2: The spell no longer counts as a [Fear] effect. The target no longer has advantage on its save.
  • Luck:
    • MP +2: If this attack inflicts a hazard, gain momentum.
    • MP +2: Augment (A) for hazard selection.
  • Mind: Change damage type to psychic.
    • MP +1: Augment (A) for hazard selection. This spell is a [Fear] effect.
  • Shadow:
    • MP +1: Attack range reduced to touch range (i.e. within your fray); peril rating increases by +5.
    • MP +1: You may choose hazards from the normal Hazard Menu.
    • MP +1: Augment (A) for hazard selection. This spell is a [Fear] effect.
