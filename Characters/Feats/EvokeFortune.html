Evoke Fortune

[Core] [Spell] Feat

Prerequisites:

Caster Level 1.

Action: Standard
Base MP Cost: 1 MP
Seeds:

Luck, Mind, Radiant

Target:

One creature within range.

Range:

Within your sector.

Base Effect:

Attempt a Spellcraft check (TN 15) to grant the target momentum. If the Spellcraft check fails, your action is forfeited, but you recycle MP equal to your Heroic Bonus (minimum 1).

Augment:
  • MP +1: Increase range to adjacent sectors.
  • MP +2: Increase range to line of sight.
  • MP +2: Cast this spell as a standard action; automatically succeed on the Spellcraft check.
  • MP +3: Target a number of creatures equal to your Spellcraft modifier. All targets must be in one sector. Make a separate check for each target to see if they receive momentum.
  • MP +4: Automatically succeed on the Spellcraft check.
Seed Effects:
  • Any (MP +1): This spell may be used regardless of active seed, but at an increased cost.
  • Luck:
    • MP +1: You may use your Awesome Check in place of any Spellcraft checks.
  • Mind:
    • MP +1: Casting this spell does not provoke opportunity attacks.
    • MP +2: Automatically succeed on your Spellcraft check, as long as you are the sole target of the spell. Casting this spell does not provoke opportunity attacks.
  • Radiant:
    • MP +0: Decrease range to touch, i.e. within your fray. Casting this spell does not provoke opportunity attacks.
