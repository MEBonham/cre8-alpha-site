Battle Banter

[Core] [Boost] Feat

Prerequisites:

Coast Number 7.

Action: Swift
Target:

One creature who can hear you, with whom you can communicate.

Trigger:

You declare a melee attack against the target.

Effect:

If the triggering attack inflicts a hazard, its target makes a Willpower save (TN equal to your default Glibness check). If this save fails, inflict an additional hazard from the provided menu.
