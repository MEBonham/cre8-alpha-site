Free Hand

An arm with a buckler attached to it can still be considered to have a free hand. An arm with a full shield attached cannot.

Stunt Attack

[Core] [Grace] [Strike] Feat

Prerequisites:

Coast Number 7; (base Reflex +2 or Brawn 3 ranks).

Action: Standard
Effect:

Move, as if taking a normal move action. As part of your movement, attempt an Athletics check, whether it is necessary or not. If your Athletics result is at least 14 (or at least 10 and you have a free hand), you may make a melee attack at the end of your movement.

If you have momentum, you may expend momentum to use your Athletics result in place of the normal accuracy for your attack.

If your attack is made with a light blade weapon, and uses your Athletics result in place of its normal accuracy, it also gains combat advantage.

Normal

A character without this feat may attempt to do a stunt attack, in order to move and attack in the same action when charging is not possible. In this case, an Athletics result of at least 22 (or 18, with a free hand) is needed, and the character must also expend momentum, for the attack to go off as part of the action.
