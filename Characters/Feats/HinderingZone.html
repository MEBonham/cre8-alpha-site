Hindering Zone

[Core] [Spell] Feat

Prerequisites:

Caster Level 1; applicable seed.

Action: Standard
Base MP Cost: 1 MP
Seeds:

Air, Cold, Earth, Fire, Lightning, Plant, Radiant, Shadow, Water, Weave

Target:

One sector or the border between two sectors, on a solid surface.

Range:

Within or bordering your sector.

Duration:

Encounter

Base Effect:

The target space is filled with a [Zone] effect that counts as difficult terrain. If the target space is a border between two sectors, then any creature moving betwee the two is affected by the difficult terrain.

Augment:
  • MP +1: Increase range to include adjacent sectors and their borders.
  • MP +1: Crossing the target (between two sectors) requires a Brawn check (TN 10).
  • MP +1 (cumulative): Add an additional target. All targets must be contiguous. Affecting more than three targets requires a Spellcraft check (TN 20); if this check fails, the additional MP beyong three targets are forfeit.
  • MP +2: Increase range to include two sectors away and their borders.
  • MP +2: The requirements for a solid surface are waived. This [Zone], unlike most difficult terrain, affects flying creatures.
  • MP +2: Increase duration to 1 day.
  • MP +3: Increase duration to 3 days.
  • MP +4: Increase duration to 1 year.
  • MP +5: Increase duration to 100 years.
Seed Effects:
  • Air (MP +1): The requirements for a solid surface are waived. This [Zone], unlike most difficult terrain, affects flying creatures.
    • MP +2: When a creature enters the [Zone] for the first time on its turn, it takes incidental bludgeoning damage equal to your Heroic Bonus.
    • MP +1: The incidental damage is applied to creatures starting their turn in the [Zone].
  • Cold: When a creature enters the [Zone] for the first time on its turn, it takes incidental cold damage equal to your Heroic Bonus.
    • MP +1: The incidental damage is applied to creatures starting their turn in the [Zone].
    • MP +2: The [Zone] is a physical barrier of ice between two sectors, which uses your Spellcraft check as its Structural Save and has an AV of (1 + your Heroic Bonus).
  • Earth: The target must be on a surface of earth or stone, and cannot use the augment to affect flying creatures.
    • MP +1: The requirement of earth or stone is waived, and the augment can be used normally.
    • MP +3: The [Zone] is a physical barrier between two sectors, which uses your Spellcraft check as its Structural Save and has an AV of (3 + your Heroic Bonus).
  • Fire: When a creature enters the [Zone] for the first time on its turn, it takes incidental fire damage equal to your Heroic Bonus.
    • MP +1: The incidental damage is applied to creatures starting their turn in the [Zone].
  • Lightning (MP +1): When a creature enters the [Zone] for the first time on its turn, it takes incidental lightning damage equal to your Heroic Bonus.
    • MP +1: The incidental damage is applied to creatures starting their turn in the [Zone].
  • Plant: The target area must be where plants could (or do) grow, and cannot use the augment to affect flying creatures. When a creature enters the [Zone] for the first time on its turn, it takes incidental bludgeoning/slashing damage equal to your Heroic Bonus.
    • MP +1: The incidental damage is applied to creatures starting their turn in the [Zone].
    • MP +1: The requirement of a plant-friendly surface is waived, and the augment can be used normally.
    • MP +2: The [Zone] is a physical barrier of plants between two sectors, which uses your Spellcraft check as its Structural Save and has an AV of (1 + your Heroic Bonus).
  • Radiant (MP +1): When a creature enters the [Zone] for the first time on its turn, it takes incidental radiance damage equal to your Heroic Bonus.
    • MP +1: The incidental damage is applied to creatures starting their turn in the [Zone].
  • Shadow (MP +1): When a creature enters the [Zone] for the first time on its turn, it takes incidental necrotic damage equal to your Heroic Bonus.
    • MP +1: The incidental damage is applied to creatures starting their turn in the [Zone].
  • Water: Creatures with the swim movement mode may ignore the difficult terrain and/or Brawn check.
    • MP +1: Only your allies with the swim movement mode may ignore the difficult terrain and/or Brawn check.
  • Weave (MP +1): When a creature enters the [Zone] for the first time on its turn, it takes incidental slashing damage equal to your Heroic Bonus.
    • MP +1: The incidental damage is applied to creatures starting their turn in the [Zone].
    • MP +4: The [Zone] is a physical barrier of force between two sectors, which uses your Spellcraft check as its Structural Save and has an AV of 10.
