Tactical Blink

[Core] [Spell] Feat

Prerequisites:

Caster Level 3; applicable seed.

Action: Standard
Base MP Cost: 1 MP
Seeds:

Air, Earth, Fire, Lightning, Luck, Mind, Plant, Radiant, Shadow, Water, Weave

Target:

Self

Range:

Within your sector or adjacent sectors.

Base Effect:

Teleport to a location within range. You must be able to see the destination, and have line of effect to it.

Augment:
  • MP +1: Cast this spell as a move action.
  • MP +1: You do not need line of effect to the destination.
  • MP +1 (cumulative): Range increases by one sector.
  • MP +2: Change the target to a willing ally, designated by touch (i.e. within your fray).
  • MP +2: The requirement to see the destination is waived.
  • MP +3: Cast this spell as a swift action.
Seed Effects:
  • Air: Casting this spell with the Air seed works normally.
  • Earth: The origin and destination must both be adjacent to the same sizable body of earth or stone.
    • MP +2: The origin and destination may be adjacent to separate bodies of earth or stone.
  • Fire:
    • MP +2: Cast this spell as an opportunity action in response to an attack targeting you. Your destination must be in the fray of the triggering attacker.
  • Lightning (MP +1): Deal incidental lightning damage, equal to the total MP cost of this spell, to all creatures in the origin and destination frays (except the teleporting creature).
    • MP +1: Only foes take the incidental lightning damage.
  • Mind: Casting this spell with the Mind seed works normally.
  • Plant: The origin and destination must each be adjacent to a plant body at least as big as the teleporting creature.
  • Radiant (MP +1): Casting this spell with the Radiant seed works normally, but at an increased MP cost.
  • Shadow: The requirement to see the destination is waived. The origin and destination cannot be in bright light.
    • MP +1: The origin can be in bright light.
    • MP +1: The destination can be in bright light.
    • MP +2: Cast this spell as a swift action. Its range increases by 1 sector.
  • Water: The origin and destination must both be adjacent to the same sizable body of water.
    • MP +1: The origin and destination may be adjacent to separate bodies of water.
  • Weave: Casting this spell with the Weave seed works normally.
