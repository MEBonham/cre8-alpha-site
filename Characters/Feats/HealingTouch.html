Healing Touch

[Core] [Spell] Feat

Prerequisites:

Caster Level 1; Charisma 2 ranks; applicable seed.

Action: Standard
Base MP Cost: 1 MP
Seeds:

Animal, Luck, Mind, Plant, Radiant, Shadow, Water

Target:

One creature within range.

Range:

Touch (i.e. within your fray).

Base Effect:

If the target is dropped, it loses the dropped condition (but not the prone condition). If the target is not dropped, it heals VP equal to half its VP Pool.

If the target is dying, it also becomes stable.

Augment:
  • MP +1: The target, if not dying, can attempt a Fortitude save (TN 20) to lose the wounded condition.
  • MP +2: The target loses the dropped condition and heals VP equal to half its VP Pool.
  • MP +2: If the target would heal half its VP Pool, it instead heals all VP damage.
  • MP +2: The target can attempt a Fortitude save (TN 20) to lose the dying condition.
  • MP +2: Casting this spell does not provoke opportunity attacks.
  • MP +3: The target, if not dying, loses the wounded condition.
  • MP +3: The target loses the dropped condition and heals all VP.
  • MP +4: Target a number of creatures equal to your Spellcraft modifier. All targets must be in one sector.
  • MP +4: The target loses the dying condition.
Seed Effects:
  • Animal: The target must not be undead. If you spend at least 2 MP total on the spell and the target is an animal, recycle 1 MP.
    • MP +2: If the target is willing, you may negate a [Shapeshift] effect on the target.
  • Luck (+1): Using this spell with the Luck seed works normally, but at an increased cost.
  • Mind: You must be a target of the spell.
    • MP +1: The spell may target another creature.
    • MP +2: The spell may target another creature. Increase range to within your sector.
  • Plant: The target must not be undead.
    • MP +1: The target may stand from prone as a free action without provoking opportunity attacks.
    • MP +2: Increase range to within your sector. The target must be in contact with the earth or with a plant at least comparable in size with itself.
  • Radiant: The target must not be undead.
    • MP +1: Increase range to within your sector.
    • MP +3: Target a number of creatures equal to your Spellcraft modifier. All targets must be in one sector.
    • MP +5: You may target a corpse that has been dead for less than an encounter. If you succeed on a TN 25 Spellcraft check, this target loses the dead condition before applying the other effects of the spell.
  • Shadow: The target must be undead or Blighted.
  • Water: The target must not be undead.
    • MP +1: Increase range to within your sector.
    • MP +1: The target may be undead.
    • MP +1: The target, if not dying, can attempt a Fortitude save (TN 20) to lose the wounded condition. You may perform a first aid check in place of this Fortitude save.
    • MP +2: The target can attempt a Fortitude save (TN 20) to lose the dying condition. You may perform a first aid check in place of this Fortitude save.
    • MP +3: Target a number of creatures equal to your Spellcraft modifier. All targets must be in one sector.
    • MP +6: You may target a corpse that has been dead for less than an encounter. If you succeed on a TN 25 Spellcraft check, this target loses the dead condition before applying the other effects of the spell.
