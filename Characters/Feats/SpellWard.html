Spell Ward

[Core] [Spell] Feat

Prerequisites:

Caster Level 1.

Action: Standard
Base MP Cost: 0 MP
Seeds:

Cold, Fire, Illusory, Lightning, Luck, Mind, Radiant, Shadow, Weave

Target:

One creature.

Range:

Touch (i.e. within your fray).

Duration:

Encounter, or until expended

Base Effect:

The target is warded; it may use your Spellcraft check in place of a saving throw against a spell attack. For this effect to work, you must spend 1 MP at the time of use, which does not require an action.

If the replaced save succeeds, or if you do not spend 1 MP when the target makes a Defense save, the ward ends for that target unless you spend an opportunity action to sustain it.

Augment:
  • MP +1: Cast this spell as a swift action. You must be the sole target of the spell.
  • MP +1: Increase range to within your sector.
  • MP +2: The target rolls both its own saving throw and your Spellcraft check, and uses the better of the two results.
  • MP +3: Target a number of creatures equal to your Spellcraft modifier. All targets must be within your sector.
Seed Effects:
  • Any (MP +2): This spell may be used regardless of active seed, but at an increased cost.
  • Cold: When the target uses this effect against a spell attack that deals fire damage, he can also resist the attack. This expends the ward regardless of outcome (although it can still be sustained).
    • MP +1: All effects of the ward may be used against any attack that deals fire damage.
  • Fire: When the target uses this effect against a spell attack that deals cold damage, he can also resist the attack. This expends the ward regardless of outcome (although it can still be sustained).
    • MP +1: All effects of the ward may be used against any attack that deals cold damage.
  • Illusory: The ward is only effective against [Divination] effects.
    • MP +2: The restriction to [Divination] effects is waived.
  • Lightning:
    • MP +1: When the target uses the ward, the attacker takes incidental lightning damage equal to your Heroic Bonus.
    • MP +1: All effects of the ward may be used against any attack that deals acid damage.
    • MP +1: When the target uses this effect against a spell attack that deals lightnig or thunder damage, he can also resist the attack. This expends the ward regardless of outcome (although it can still be sustained).
  • Luck: The ward is not expended by a successful use; instead, the ward is expended (unless sustained) if the target fails its save while using the effect.
  • Mind: The ward can only be used in place of a Willpower save.
    • MP +1: When you (not another target) use the ward and it would be expended, sustain it as a free action.
    • MP +1: When the target uses this effect in place of a Willpower save, he can also resist the attack. This expends the ward regardless of outcome (although it can still be sustained).
    • MP +2: The restriction to replacing Willpower saves is waived.
  • Radiant: When the target uses this effect against a spell attack that deals necrotic damage, he can also resist the attack. This expends the ward regardless of outcome (although it can still be sustained).
    • MP +1: All effects of the ward may be used against any attack that deals necrotic damage.
  • Shadow: When the target uses this effect against a spell attack that deals radiance damage, he can also resist the attack. This expends the ward regardless of outcome (although it can still be sustained).
    • MP +1: All effects of the ward may be used against any attack that deals radiance damage.
  • Weave:
    • MP +2: You may sustain the spell as a free action.
Benefit:

Gain a +1 feat bonus to your Spellcraft check.
