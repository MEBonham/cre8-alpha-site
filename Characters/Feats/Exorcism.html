Exorcism

[Spell] Feat

Prerequisites:

Caster Level 1; applicable seed.

Action: Standard
Base MP Cost: 1 MP
Seeds:

Fire, Mind, Radiant, Shadow

Target:

All Blighted creatures in your sector.

Duration:

While sustained, plus one round.

Base Effect:

Make a spell attack against the target(s): impact 1d12, damage type dependent on seed, peril +0, range 20/40/60 ft. Targets can oppose this attack with Fortitude or Willpower. Undead can take attrition damage from this attack. If this attack inflicts a hazard, select it from the provided menu instead of the normal Hazard Menu.

If any creatures in the spell's effect are [Possessed] by Blighted creatures, this spell has the capability to target the possessing entity without harming the [Possessed] creature.

Augment:
  • MP +1: If this attack inflicts a hazard, select a hazard from the normal Hazard Menu in addition to a hazard from the menu provided here.
  • MP +2 (cumulative): Add an additional sector, adjacent to your sector, to the area affected by this spell.
  • MP +2: Target a single Blighted creature instead of all Blighted creatures in an area. Add +1d4 impact.
Seed Effects:
  • Fire (MP +1): The attack deals fire damage.
  • Mind: The attack deals psychic damage.
    • MP +5: Any possessing creature is automatically expelled from their shell, regardless of what other effects the attack has on them.
  • Radiant: The attack deals radiance damage. The attack deals +1d4 impact to undead creatures, although this does not stack with any vulnerability to radiance damage that they may have normally.
  • Shadow: The attack deals necrotic damage.
Sustain:

Standard action; spend 1 MP.
