Energy Strike

[Core] [Spell] [Strike] Feat

Prerequisites:

Caster Level 1; applicable seed.

Action: Standard
Base MP Cost: 1 MP
Seeds:

Air, Cold, Fire, Lightning, Mind, Radiant, Shadow, Weave

Base Effect:

Make a melee weapon attack. It adds +1d6 impact. Casting this spell does not provoke opportunity attacks. The somatic component of this spell can be performed by a hand that is wielding a weapon.

Augment:
  • MP +1: Make a ranged weapon attack instead of a melee attack.
  • MP +2: Add +2d4 impact instead of 1d6.
Seed Effects:
  • Air (MP +1): The attack deals bludgeoning damage, in addition to its normal damage type.
    • MP +1: Your melee attack may target a creature outside your fray (still in your sector).
  • Cold: The attack deals cold damage, in addition to its normal damage type.
    • MP +1: This attack deals only cold damage (not its normal damage type).
  • Fire: The attack deals fire damage, in addition to its normal damage type.
    • MP +1: Your melee attack may target a creature outside your fray (still in your sector).
    • MP +1: This attack deals only fire damage (not its normal damage type).
  • Lightning: The attack deals lightning damage, in addition to its normal damage type.
    • MP +1: This attack deals lightning and thunder damage (not its normal damage type).
    • MP +2: This attack deals only lightning or only thunder damage, your choice.
  • Mind (MP +1): Using this spell with the Mind seed works normally, but at an increased cost.
  • Radiant: The attack deals radiance damage, in addition to its normal damage type.
    • MP +1: This attack deals only radiance damage (not its normal damage type).
  • Shadow: The attack deals necrotic damage, in addition to its normal damage type.
    • MP +1: This attack deals only necrotic damage (not its normal damage type).
  • Weave (MP +1): Using this spell with the Weave seed works normally, but at an increased cost.
