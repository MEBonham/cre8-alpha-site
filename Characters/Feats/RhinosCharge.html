Rhino's Charge

[Core] [Strike] Feat

Prerequisites:

Base Fortitude +2; Athletics 1 rank; Brawn 3 ranks.

Action: Standard (expend momentum)
Effect:

Make a charge attack. Immediately after this charge attack is resolved, attempt to shove the target away as a free action.

If your charge attack did not inflict a hazard and your shove was unsuccessful, retain momentum.

Benefit:

You do not provoke opportunity attacks by shoving.
