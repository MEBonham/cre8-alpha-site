All Feats

Following is a list of all the Feats in the game, even the ones intended for the GM only.

Anyfeat
amazing flexibility at dabbling in many stunts
Anyspell
make magic do many things, with enough power
Battle Banter
goad your foes into fighting poorly
Bestow Curse
lay a magical curse on an enemy
Call Ally
magically create or summon a friendly helper
Cleave
slice through one foe and into another
Commander's Urge
give allies early turns with your leadership skills
Dazing Strike
hit an enemy so they see stars
Deadeye Shot
make a dramatic perfect ranged attack
Death Attack
instantly slay an unaware foe
Dirty Trick
classic "sand in the eyes" or similar tactics
Dispel
magically counter other magical effects
Disrupting Shot
hinder a foe's actions with well-timed ranged attack
Dodge
swiftly get out of the way, rather than blocking
Energy Blast
attack with magical blasts from a distance
Energy Strike
infuse a weapon attack with extra magical energy
Evoke Fortune
magically bless an ally with good fortune
Exorcism
turn away creatures influenced by Blight
Expert Parry
deflect an attack especially well
Flurry Attack
strike several times in rapid succession
Furious Rage
enter an angry trance that helps shrug off pain
Glamer
freely shape illusionary figments
Guardian's Rebuke
punish a foe for attempting to harm your ally
Healing Touch
magical healing
Heroic Surge
gain a key extra action
Hindering Zone
magically alter terrain to hinder enemy combatants
Improvisation
perform skills especially well under dramatic pressure
Lightfoot Step
stance to move about difficult terrain smoothly
Mage Flight
magically fly
Mind Crush
magically assault a foe mind-to-mind
Power Attack
put extra oomph into an attack
Precise Shot
effectively make ranged attacks in impractical situations
Radiate Aura
aid allies with your very presence
Rallying Strike
striking a foe can impassion you to fight harder afterwards
Rhino's Charge
charge with enough force to send the target flying
Shield Block
protect a nearby ally with your shield
Smite
infuse an attack with subtle mystic power
Sneak Attack
stab an unaware foe especially effectively
Sniper's Shot
shoot effectively from a hidden location
Spell Ward
magically protect an ally from harmful magic
Stunt Attack
perform dramatic acrobatic stunts while attacking
Swarm Assault
attack between your turns when you outnumber your foe
Tactical Blink
magically move instantaneously to a new location
Thicket of Blades
make the area around you especially dangerous
Toughness
get a second wind partway through a fight
Transformation
magically shapeshift
Two-Weapon Style
fight effectively with a weapon in each hand
Two-Weapon Tempest
fight very well with both hands in a flurry of destruction
Vanish
magically disappear
Veteran's Grit
determinedly throw off adverse conditions
Weapon Ward
magically protect an ally from physical harm
Winged Flight
fly
Words of Motivation
inspire an ally to keep fighting beyond their normal limits
