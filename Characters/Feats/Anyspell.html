Anyspell

[Core] [Spell] Feat

Prerequisites:

Caster Level 4; three [Seed] talents.

Action:

Varies (as duplicated spell)

Base MP Cost:

Varies (as duplicated spell +1)

Seeds:

Varies (as duplicated spell)

Effect:

Duplicate the effect of any other [Spell] feat as if you were casting it normally, except with an additional 1 MP cost. This additional 1 MP counts toward your channeling limit.

Augment:
  • MP +1: Cast the duplicated spell using a seed that is not active, or even a seed you don't have.
