Dispel

[Core] [Spell] Feat

Prerequisites:

Caster Level 2.

Action: Standard
Base MP Cost: 1 MP
Seeds:

Shadow, Weave

Target:

One [Zone] effect, one [Conjuration] effect, or all active spell effects on one creature.

Range:

Within your sector.

Base Effect:

Attempt a Spellcraft check to end each affected target (TN 10 + source's Spellcraft modifier).

Augment:
  • MP +1 (cumulative): Add an additional target (all effects on one creature count as one target). All targets must be within one sector.
  • MP +1: Increase range to include adjacent sectors.
  • MP +2: Increase range to within two sectors.
  • MP +3: Increase range to line of sight.
Seed Effects:
  • Any (MP +1): This spell may be used regardless of active seed, but at an increased cost.
  • Shadow (MP +1): The originator of each dispelled effect takes incidental necrotic damage equal to the MP spent on this spell. A given creature can only be affected by this once per casting of Dispel.
  • Weave: Using this spell with the Weave Seed works normally.
Benefit:

Gain a +1 feat bonus to your Spellcraft check.
