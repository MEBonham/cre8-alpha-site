Stooges

Stooges are generic monsters that are quick and easy to use, intended to fill supporting roles such as summoned creatures. Stooges use a common set of underlying stats, but then can be customized by a number of menu options that give them low-bookkeeping special abilities.

When you need to represent a larger number of supporting creatures, you can also use stooge swarms.
